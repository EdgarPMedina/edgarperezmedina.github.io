About Me

I am a Mexican economist and currently a Master's student in Economics at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), where I also earned my Bachelor's degree with a specialization in Monetary and Financial Economics. My research interests lie at the intersection of macroeconomic policy, financial stability, and economic growth, particularly in emerging markets.

My professional experience includes serving as a research assistant for leading economists and collaborating as an external consultant for institutions such as UNCTAD, INEGI, and the Espinosa Yglesias Study Center.
